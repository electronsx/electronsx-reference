# Chinese Electric Vehicle Brands

This document lists battery-electric vehicle (BEV) brands originating from the People’s Republic of China.

China represents the world’s largest EV manufacturing base and accounts for the majority of global BEV production.

**Scope**

- China-headquartered brands only  
- BEV-focused (no hybrids or ICE-only marques)  
- Includes OEM-owned sub-brands marketed independently  
- Excludes trims, concept-only labels, and export rebadges  

Last updated: 2026-01  

Maintained by **ElectronsX**  
Full datasets and analytics: https://electronsx.com
